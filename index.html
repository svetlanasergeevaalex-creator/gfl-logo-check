Logo Check

Upload a hotel logo to check it against Google Hotel Center requirements and prepare a corrected PNG. Everything runs locally in your browser — the file is never uploaded anywhere.

Requirements

1 File format

  • PNG only. JPG, JPEG, SVG, WEBP and others are rejected.

2 Image size

  • Allowed range: 72×72 to 1200×1200 px.
  • Prepare 512×512 (optimal) or 1024×1024 (extra quality). Google rescales automatically.

3 Proportions

  • Strict square, 1:1 — e.g. 512×512.
  • Non-square images such as 512×300 are rejected.

4 Background

  • Transparent (preferred) or a coloured background.
  • A white logo on white, or any logo that disappears against Google's white interface.

5 Text inside the logo

  • Up to 3 characters — e.g. H, HB, XYZ.
  • Full names like "Grand Palace Hotel" are usually rejected for exceeding the limit.

6 Brand consistency

  • The logo must match the brand on the hotel's official site, favicon and corporate identity. Google compares the upload to the brand on the final URL and may reject mismatches.
